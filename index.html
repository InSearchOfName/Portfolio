About Me

Hi, I’m Tommy Quatretemps (AKA InSearchOfName), currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Applied Computer Science at PXL University of Applied Sciences and Arts, specializing in Systems and Network Engineering. I have a strong interest in cloud management and Linux administration, and while I previously managed my personal Linux server, I have for now transitioned to cloud environments. My journey into cloud computing started when I set up my first Minecraft server, sparking a deep fascination with server management, networking, and cloud infrastructure.

I also continue to develop small projects, such as Minecraft plugins, to keep exploring and practicing my technical skills.
